About

The Cinematics began as the brainchild of two passionate and creative individuals Cole Meyer and H. Thomas Altman in the fall of 2017. Dedicated to the development of quality feature-length films for the market and thrilling experiences for the viewer, the Cinematics embarked on their first journey with the feature-film Chaste, a drama written by Cole Meyer, in which, a womanizer must overcome his inner-demons in which he pursuit of the love of his life.Nearing post-production, the film is set for release soon and to be distributed for streaming and purchase later. On the horizon, the Cinematics have various projects lined up and being currently written and conceptualized for the cinema and beyond.

Cole Meyer

Cole Meyer is a screenwriter, producer, director, editor, camera operator and actor. Ever since he was a small child, Cole has had an interest in film. Discovering over time that he was a storyteller and had a strong visual eye, Cole decided to pursue an AAS Degree in Digital Video Production and BA in Screenwriting. After graduating, Cole started to enter contests and film festivals, using his God-given talents and knowledge he received from his years of school and earning accolades, awards and recognition. When Cole turned 30, he decided that it was time to check off the ultimate dream on his bucket list: make a feature-film. Debating whether or not he could do it for years, meeting with H. Thomas Altman, his business partner of the Cinematics, they decided to team up and produce and make Chaste together. Now, from here, the sky is the limit of where the Cinematics can go and what they have planned for the future.

H. thomas Altman

H. Thomas Altman is an American actor, screenwriter and producer and creator of his own comic book series Strong Box. He has appeared on nationally syndicated television shows such as the History Channel's Monster Quest series and Shop NBC. His feature-leading roles include: Chaste, Max Bishop, Into the Void, Insectula, the Center, the Devil's Hour, Christmas Ride, Not Quite Lyin' Eyes, Gray State, and Ash Land the Series.
